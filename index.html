What We Do

Our Services

From residential garages to sprawling commercial warehouses — every surface gets our full attention and the finest materials available.

01
Garage Floor Epoxy

Transform your garage into a showroom-quality space with seamless, chemical-resistant epoxy that stands up to tire marks, oil, and heavy vehicles.

  • Solid color & metallic finishes
  • Flake chip broadcast systems
  • Anti-slip top coats
  • UV-stable formulations
02
Residential Interiors

Basements, laundry rooms, workshops — seamless epoxy creates a clean, modern look that's easy to maintain and impressively durable.

  • Polished concrete aesthetics
  • Moisture vapor barriers
  • Custom color matching
  • Low-VOC options available
03
Commercial & Industrial

High-traffic commercial environments demand the toughest coatings. Our industrial-grade systems handle forklifts, chemicals, and constant foot traffic.

  • Warehouse & factory floors
  • Retail & showroom spaces
  • Restaurant & kitchen floors
  • OSHA-compliant safety markings
04
Metallic Epoxy Systems

The pinnacle of decorative flooring — three-dimensional metallic effects that shift and shimmer with the light, creating a truly one-of-a-kind surface.

  • Pearl & mica pigments
  • Marble & lava effects
  • Galaxy & ocean themes
  • High-gloss clear coat finish
05
Surface Preparation & Repair

The foundation of any lasting epoxy installation. We diamond-grind, acid-etch, and repair cracks before any coating is applied.

  • Diamond grinding & profiling
  • Crack & spall repair
  • Moisture testing & mitigation
  • Shot blasting available
06
Handyman & Flooring Services

Beyond epoxy — our skilled team handles a range of flooring and handyman tasks to help you complete your space from top to bottom.

  • General concrete work
  • Minor flooring repairs
  • Pressure washing & prep
  • Sealing & waterproofing
